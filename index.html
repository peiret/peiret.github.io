Welcome to my site, where you can learn about me and my career as an engineer. Don't forget to check out my research.

I'm currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Research Centre for Biomedical Engineering in the Mediterranean city of Barcelona, where I grew up. I graduated from the Barcelona School of Industrial Engineering of UPC, where I now teach part-time several courses to engineering students at both Bachelor's and Master's levels.

I did my PhD in Mechanical Engineering at McGill Univeristy in Montréal, Canada. I cunducted my research in the Applied Dynamics Lab, which was mainly focused on the simulation of mechanical systems, such as robots, vehicles, and industrial machinery.
